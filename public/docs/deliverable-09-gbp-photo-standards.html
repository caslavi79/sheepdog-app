How to Name Files

Rename every photo before uploading. Use this format:

sheepdog-[service]-[what it is]-[city]-[YYYY-MM].jpg

All lowercase. Hyphens, no spaces. Always include the city. Examples:

sheepdog-event-security-door-college-station-2026-04.jpg
sheepdog-bartending-setup-bryan-tx-2026-04.jpg
sheepdog-staffing-warehouse-brazos-valley-2026-05.jpg
sheepdog-team-group-bryan-college-station-2026-04.jpg

Upload Schedule

Launch

Logo + cover + 3 best photos. Right away. Don't wait.

Month 1

2-3 photos a week until you hit 10. Events shots first.

Month 2-3

1-2 a week. Fill Staffing and Gear categories. Hit 15+.

After That

1 new photo every 2 weeks minimum. Grab one at every gig.

Photo Rules

OrientationLandscape (sideways). Always. GBP crops vertical photos badly.
FlashOff. Stand near a light source instead.
FiltersNone. No Instagram filters, no black and white, no edits.
FocusTap your screen on the subject before you shoot. If it's blurry, retake it.
BrandingAt least half your photos should have something Sheepdog-branded in them.
File size10KB to 5MB. Phone photos are fine as-is.
File nameRename it using the format above. Not IMG_4823.jpg.
These same rules and naming conventions apply to social media and the website. One set of standards, all channels.
