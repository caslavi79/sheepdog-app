Instructions

  • Complete a W-9 form (IRS Form W-9) and return it to Sheepdog before your first assignment.
  • You can download the official W-9 form at irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-w-9.
  • Email your completed W-9 to sheepdogsecurityllc@gmail.com or hand it to your team lead.
  • No payments will be issued until a valid W-9 is on file.
  • Submit within 7 days of signing your Independent Contractor Agreement. Assignments will not be offered until this is received.

Important

Your SSN/EIN will be kept confidential and used solely for IRS tax reporting. Sheepdog does not share this information with clients, venues, or any third party.
