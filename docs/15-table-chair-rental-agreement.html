Damage & Loss

Client is responsible for all equipment from delivery to pickup

  • Client assumes full responsibility for the rented equipment from the moment of delivery until it is picked up by Sheepdog.
  • Normal wear (minor scuffs, surface dirt) is expected and will not be charged.
  • Damage beyond normal wear (broken legs, torn seats, bent frames, stains on linens, burn marks, water damage) will be charged at replacement cost.
  • Lost or stolen items will be charged at full replacement cost.
  • Replacement costs are listed on the Equipment Inventory Checklist. If not listed, Sheepdog will provide documentation of replacement cost.
  • Damage and loss charges will be invoiced within 7 days of pickup and are due within 15 days.

Damage Deposit[DAMAGE DEPOSIT]

If a damage deposit is collected, it will be refunded in full within 7 days of pickup if all equipment is returned in acceptable condition. If damage or loss occurs, the cost will be deducted from the deposit first, and any remaining balance will be invoiced separately.

Client Responsibilities

  • Ensure the delivery location is accessible for Sheepdog's delivery vehicle (clear path, adequate space, no locked gates without prior arrangement).
  • If Client is setting up equipment, do so on level, stable ground. Do not use equipment on wet, muddy, or uneven surfaces without Sheepdog's approval.
  • Do not modify, paint, nail into, glue, tape, or permanently alter any rented equipment.
  • Do not stand on tables or chairs, use chairs as step ladders, or exceed reasonable weight limits. Damage from misuse is billed at full replacement cost.
  • Stack chairs and fold/collapse tables for pickup unless Sheepdog is providing breakdown service.
  • Keep equipment in a secure location. Equipment left outdoors overnight is the Client's risk.
  • Linens should be returned as-is (do not wash). Sheepdog handles cleaning. Linens with permanent stains (wax, paint, bleach, burns) will be charged at replacement cost.

Equipment Ownership

  • All rented equipment remains the sole property of Sheepdog Security & Staffing LLC at all times.
  • Client may not sublease, lend, transfer, or allow any third party to use the equipment outside of the event described in this agreement.
  • Client has no lien, ownership claim, or right of retention over any rented equipment. Equipment may not be held to offset any dispute.

Equipment Substitution

If a specific item listed in this agreement becomes unavailable (due to prior damage, inventory shortage, or other circumstances), Sheepdog will notify the Client and offer a comparable substitute at no additional charge. If the Client does not accept the substitute, the item will be removed from the order and the rental fee adjusted accordingly.

Difficult Access

Standard delivery assumes ground-level, truck-accessible drop-off. If the delivery location requires stairs, elevators, long carries (100+ feet from vehicle), or other non-standard access, an additional delivery labor fee of [DIFFICULT ACCESS FEE] may apply. Sheepdog will notify the Client of this fee before delivery if known in advance.

Delivery & Pickup

  • Sheepdog will deliver equipment to the location specified above during the agreed time window.
  • Client or Client's designated on-site contact must be present at delivery and pickup to sign the inventory checklist.
  • If no one is present at delivery, Sheepdog may leave equipment at the designated location, and Client assumes responsibility from that point. The delivery portion of the checklist will be marked as unsigned.
  • If equipment is not accessible at the scheduled pickup time, a re-pickup fee applies (see above).

Cancellation Policy

  • 14+ days before delivery: deposit forfeited, no additional charges.
  • 7-13 days before delivery: Client owes 50% of rental fee.
  • Within 7 days of delivery: Client owes 100% of rental fee.

Force Majeure

  • Neither party is liable for failure to perform due to events beyond reasonable control, including but not limited to: severe weather, natural disasters, government orders, public health emergencies, venue closures, or civil disturbances.
  • If a force majeure event prevents the event from occurring, the Client's deposit will be applied as a credit toward a future booking within 12 months. If no rebooking occurs within 12 months, the deposit is forfeited.
  • Sheepdog will make reasonable efforts to notify the Client as soon as possible if it cannot fulfill the agreement due to a force majeure event.

Liability & Insurance

  • Sheepdog maintains general liability insurance covering delivery and pickup operations.
  • Sheepdog is not liable for injuries caused by improper use, setup, or placement of rented equipment by the Client or their guests.
  • Client agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Sheepdog from any claims arising from the use of rented equipment at the event, except where caused by defective equipment that Sheepdog knew or should have known about.
  • If Sheepdog identifies equipment as unsafe before or during delivery, Sheepdog will replace it or remove it from the order and adjust the invoice accordingly.
