Response Rules

  • Respond within 24-48 hours. Every review gets a reply. No exceptions.
  • Use their name. Google shows the reviewer's first name. Use it.
  • Reference specifics when you can. The venue, event type, or service.
  • Keep it to 2-4 sentences.
  • One person owns responses. Same person every time.
  • Never: Get defensive, argue in public, offer discounts to change a review, or reveal event details.

Positive Reviews (5 Stars)

★★★★★

Thank them, mention what went right, keep the door open.

Variation 1 - Events client (venue/bar)

Response

Thanks so much, [Name]! We really enjoyed working [Venue/Event] with your team. Making sure everything runs smooth is what we're all about, so it's great to hear we hit the mark. Looking forward to the next one.

Variation 2 - Events client (wedding/private)

Response

Thank you, [Name]! Your [event type, e.g. wedding, celebration] was a special one to be part of, and we're glad we could help make the night go off without a hitch. Means a lot that you took the time to share this. All the best to you and yours.

Variation 3 - Staffing / corporate client

Response

Appreciate the feedback, [Name]. Reliability and follow-through are the standard we hold ourselves to on every placement, so it's good to hear that came through with your team at [Company]. We look forward to continuing the partnership.

Neutral Reviews (3-4 Stars)

★★★★☆

Acknowledge the good part. Address the issue. Don't be defensive.

Variation 1 - They liked the service but had a specific complaint

Response

Thanks for the feedback, [Name]. Glad [positive aspect] went well. We hear you on [issue] and that's something we want to get right every time. If you're up for it, reach out at (979) 204-0945 or sheepdogsecurityllc@gmail.com so we can make it better next round.

Variation 2 - Vague / no specifics given

Response

Thanks for the review, [Name]. We want every experience to be a five-star one, so if there's anything specific we could have done better, we'd genuinely like to know. You can reach us anytime at (979) 204-0945 or sheepdogsecurityllc@gmail.com.

Negative Reviews (1-2 Stars)

★★☆☆☆

Don't argue. Don't make excuses. Take it offline. Everyone reading this review is reading your response too.

Variation 1 - Specific complaint (late arrival, staff issue, etc.)

Response

[Name], thanks for letting us know. That's not the standard we hold ourselves to. We'd like to make this right. Please reach out at (979) 204-0945 or sheepdogsecurityllc@gmail.com so we can talk through it.

Variation 2 - Angry / unfair / possibly wrong client

Response

[Name], we appreciate you sharing this and we take every review seriously. We'd like to better understand your experience and see if there's something we can do. Please feel free to contact us at (979) 204-0945 or sheepdogsecurityllc@gmail.com.

Negative Review Playbook

  • Don't respond right away. Step away for an hour first.
  • Never reveal event details - don't make the client look bad publicly.
  • Move it offline. Every time.
  • Fake or spam? Flag it to Google via "Report review." Don't engage.
  • After resolving offline: You can politely ask if they'd update their review. Never pressure.
