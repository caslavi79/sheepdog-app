TABC Compliance

  • All Sheepdog bartenders hold valid TABC certifications and will carry proof of certification on-site.
  • Bartenders will refuse service to any visibly intoxicated person in accordance with Texas law.
  • Bartenders will check IDs for all guests who appear under 30 years of age.
  • Sheepdog reserves the right to shut down bar service at any time if continued service would violate TABC regulations or pose a safety risk.

Alcohol Liability

  • If alcohol is provided by the Client, the Client assumes responsibility for the quality and legality of all beverages supplied.
  • Sheepdog is not liable for the actions of intoxicated guests after they leave the bar area.
  • Client agrees to indemnify Sheepdog from any claims related to alcohol consumption at the event, except where caused by Sheepdog's direct negligence.

Permits & Licensing

  • Client is responsible for obtaining any event-specific alcohol permits required by Texas law or the venue.
  • Sheepdog does not hold a liquor license and does not sell alcohol. Sheepdog provides bartending labor only.
  • If the event requires a temporary alcohol permit, the Client must have it on-site before bar service begins. Sheepdog will not begin service without it.

Bar Equipment & Cleanup

  • If Sheepdog provides bar equipment, Client is responsible for any damage or loss to that equipment during the event that is not caused by Sheepdog staff.
  • Sheepdog bartenders will clean and break down the bar area at the end of service. General venue cleanup beyond the bar area is the Client's responsibility.

Force Majeure

  • Neither party is liable for failure to perform due to events beyond reasonable control, including but not limited to: severe weather, natural disasters, government orders, public health emergencies, venue closures, or civil disturbances.
  • If a force majeure event prevents the event from occurring, the Client's deposit will be applied as a credit toward a future booking within 12 months. If no rebooking occurs within 12 months, the deposit is forfeited.
  • Sheepdog will make reasonable efforts to notify the Client as soon as possible if it cannot fulfill the agreement due to a force majeure event.

On-Site Changes

  • If the Client requests additional staff, extended hours, or expanded scope on the day of the event, Sheepdog will make reasonable efforts to accommodate.
  • Additional hours or staff will be billed at the rates listed in this agreement.
  • Verbal on-site approvals from the Client's designated contact are considered binding for billing purposes.
