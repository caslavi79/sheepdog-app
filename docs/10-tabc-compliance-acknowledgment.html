Acknowledgment

By signing below, I acknowledge and agree to the following:

  1. I hold a valid Texas Alcoholic Beverage Commission (TABC) seller/server certification and it is current as of the date of this form.
  2. I will carry proof of my TABC certification on my person during any assignment that involves alcohol service.
  3. I will comply with all TABC regulations and Texas alcohol service laws while on assignment, including but not limited to:
    • Refusing service to visibly intoxicated persons.
    • Checking IDs for anyone who appears under 30 years of age.
    • Not serving minors under any circumstances.
  4. I understand that Sheepdog may verify my TABC certification at any time and that I must provide updated proof if my certification is renewed.
  5. If my TABC certification expires or is revoked, I will notify Sheepdog immediately and understand that I will not be assigned to any bartending or alcohol-service work until a valid certification is on file.
  6. If a client, venue manager, or any other person pressures me to serve someone I have cut off or to violate any TABC regulation, I will refuse and immediately contact Sheepdog management.
  7. Sheepdog will support any bartender who refuses service in compliance with TABC, even if the client objects.
  8. Failure to comply with TABC regulations while on assignment may result in immediate removal from the assignment and termination of my contractor agreement with Sheepdog.
