01 - Brand Positioning
What Sheepdog Staffing Is

One paragraph. What it is, who it serves, why it's different. Every piece of Staffing marketing traces back to this.

Sheepdog Staffing is the contracted labor and workforce solutions arm of Sheepdog, providing vetted, dependable workers for companies that need personnel at scale. We serve businesses across industries - from GPS tagging deployments and field logistics to facility maintenance, warehouse operations, and project-based labor. Our clients are companies, HR departments, operations managers, and project leads who need reliable people on-site without the overhead of hiring, vetting, and managing them directly. What makes us different: we handle everything end-to-end - recruiting, background checks, onboarding paperwork, scheduling, and compliance - so the client gets a fully managed workforce with a single point of contact. You define the scope. We deliver the people.

!

Critical distinction: Sheepdog Staffing is not limited to security personnel. It is a general contracted labor service. While the parent brand has roots in security, the Staffing line serves any company that needs workers deployed at scale for any task. Copy should never box the offering into "security staffing."

What We Are vs. What We Aren't

What We Are

  • A managed workforce provider - we recruit, vet, deploy, and manage
  • A single point of contact for contracted labor at any scale
  • Industry-flexible - field ops, logistics, facilities, warehouse, project labor
  • Compliance-handled - W-9s, background checks, insurance, onboarding
  • A partner that scales up and down with the client's needs

What We Aren't

  • A temp app or gig platform - we don't do marketplace matching
  • A staffing agency that sends resumes for you to sort through
  • Limited to security - we staff any contracted labor role
  • A body shop - every worker is vetted, briefed, and managed by us
  • A one-off provider - we build ongoing workforce relationships
02 - Brand Voice
How Sheepdog Staffing Sounds

Four attributes. This is a corporate audience - operations managers, HR leads, project directors. The voice should make them feel like they're working with a capable, professional partner.

Professional
Buttoned-up, polished, competent. We speak the language of businesses - deliverables, timelines, compliance, SLAs.
✓ Do

"We deploy vetted personnel within 48 hours of contract execution, with full documentation provided on day one."

✗ Don't

"Just hit us up and we'll get some guys out to your site ASAP."

Reliable
The single most important thing a staffing partner can be. Workers show up. On time. Every shift. No gaps, no excuses.
✓ Do

"Zero missed shifts across 340 placement hours last quarter. That's the standard we hold ourselves to."

✗ Don't

"We do our best to make sure workers show up on time."

Efficient
We don't waste your time. Proposals are fast, onboarding is streamlined, communication is clear. Everything reduces friction for the client.
✓ Do

"Scope received Monday. Proposal delivered Tuesday. Workers on-site Thursday."

✗ Don't

"Once we receive your detailed requirements, our team will initiate a comprehensive evaluation process to determine the optimal staffing configuration for your needs."

No-Nonsense
We don't oversell, don't overpromise, and don't pad proposals with fluff. Clients get exactly what they asked for - clearly scoped, clearly priced, clearly delivered.
✓ Do

"12 workers, 5 days, $22/hr billed. W-9s, background checks, and workers' comp included. Here's the contract."

✗ Don't

"Our holistic workforce ecosystem leverages synergistic human capital strategies to optimize your operational throughput."

03 - Messaging Pillars
Three Themes We Always Return To

Every piece of Sheepdog Staffing marketing ties back to at least one of these.

01

Vetted & Ready

Every worker we place is background-checked, documented, and prepared for the job. We don't send warm bodies - we send people who've been vetted, briefed on the scope, and cleared to work. Compliance paperwork is handled before anyone sets foot on-site.

02

Fully Managed

Clients don't manage our workers - we do. Recruiting, vetting, onboarding, scheduling, compliance, and payroll all run through us. The client gets a single point of contact and total visibility into coverage. No juggling subcontractors. No HR overhead. One partner, fully managed.

03

Scalable Workforce

Need 3 people for a week or 30 for a quarter? We scale to match the scope. Ramp up fast, ramp down clean. From one-off field deployments to ongoing operational support, the model flexes to fit.

06 - FAQ Copy
Questions B2B Buyers Ask

Pre-written answers for the /staffing page FAQ section. Written in the Staffing voice - direct, no fluff, answers the question then stops.

What types of work do you staff for?
Anything that requires contracted workers at scale. Our most common engagements are field operations (GPS tagging, survey work), logistics and warehouse staffing, facility maintenance, and project-based labor. We're not limited to any single industry - if you need vetted people on-site, we can scope it.
How quickly can you deploy workers?
For standard engagements, we can have vetted workers on-site within 48–72 hours of contract execution. For larger deployments or specialized roles, we'll provide a timeline during the scoping process. Ongoing placements with repeating schedules are set up once and run continuously.
Do you handle background checks and compliance?
Yes - everything. Background checks, W-9 collection, independent contractor agreements, insurance verification, and any client-specific onboarding requirements. Workers are fully documented before they set foot on your site.
What's the minimum contract size?
We don't have a hard minimum, but our model is built for engagements of 3+ workers. Single-person placements can work for ongoing roles but aren't cost-effective for one-off shifts. We'll be upfront during scoping if a project isn't the right fit.
How does billing work?
We bill per worker per hour, with rates set at contract signing. You'll receive a single invoice - no separate bills from individual workers. Timesheets are tracked and verified by our on-site point of contact before invoicing. Net 15 or Net 30 terms are available.
What if a worker doesn't perform?
We replace them. Our team manages performance on-site, and if a worker isn't meeting expectations, we swap them out - typically within 24 hours. You don't need to fire or manage anyone. That's our job.
07 - Visual Expression
How Staffing Looks Different

Sheepdog Staffing shares the parent brand system but expresses it with more restraint and structure than Events. Cooler, cleaner, data-forward.

Color Emphasis

  • Navy #1B2A4A is the Staffing accent - not amber
  • Signal Red #C23B22 reserved for error states only
  • More gray and steel tones than Events
  • White and Bone backgrounds - clean, clinical separation
  • No warm tones, no cream, no amber

Typography Lean

  • H1: Barlow Semi Condensed 800, sentence case acceptable
  • Clean, measured display - less shouty than Events
  • Slightly longer sentences OK - professional vocabulary
  • Passive voice acceptable when corporate tone fits
  • CTA language: "Request Staff," "Get a Proposal," "Schedule a Call"

Imagery Direction

  • Workers on-site, logistics in action, organized operations
  • Avoid anything that looks like nightlife, events, or parties
  • Hi-vis vests, clipboards, organized teams, warehouses, corporate
  • Daytime photography - well-lit, clean, operational
  • People working, not posing - candid but corporate

Layout & Composition

  • More structured than Events - clean grids, generous whitespace
  • Data-forward where appropriate - stats, metrics, timelines
  • Fewer full-bleed images, more contained compositions
  • Cards, tables, structured layouts over large hero images
  • Information density is higher - B2B audiences scan for specifics
