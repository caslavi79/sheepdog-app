Purpose

During your engagement with Sheepdog Security & Staffing LLC ("Sheepdog"), you will have access to confidential information related to Sheepdog's business, its clients, and the venues and events where you are assigned. This agreement exists to protect that information.

What Counts as Confidential Information

  • Client names, contact information, and contract terms
  • Venue details, layouts, security protocols, and access procedures
  • Event details including guest lists, schedules, and private event information
  • Sheepdog's pricing, pay rates, and billing structures
  • Sheepdog's client lists, business strategies, and internal operations
  • Contractor and employee personal information
  • Any information marked or communicated as confidential

Your Obligations

  1. Do not share, disclose, or discuss confidential information with anyone outside of Sheepdog, including friends, family, social media, or other employers.
  2. Do not post photos, videos, or details about events, venues, or clients on social media or any public platform.
  3. Do not use confidential information for personal gain or to benefit any competing business.
  4. If you receive a legal request (subpoena, court order) for confidential information, notify Sheepdog immediately before disclosing anything.
  5. Return or delete any confidential materials (documents, files, photos) when your engagement with Sheepdog ends.

Duration

This agreement is effective as of the date signed and remains in effect during your engagement with Sheepdog and for [NDA DURATION AFTER TERMINATION - e.g. 1 year, 2 years] after your engagement ends.

Consequences of Breach

Violation of this agreement may result in immediate termination of your contractor agreement, and Sheepdog reserves the right to pursue legal remedies including damages and injunctive relief.

Exclusions

Confidential information does not include information that:

  • Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Contractor
  • Was already known to the Contractor before their engagement with Sheepdog, and Contractor can prove this with documentation
  • Is independently developed by the Contractor without use of Sheepdog's confidential information
  • Is required to be disclosed by law, court order, or government regulation, provided Contractor notifies Sheepdog first when legally permitted
  • Is shared with Contractor's own legal counsel for the purpose of receiving legal advice related to this agreement
